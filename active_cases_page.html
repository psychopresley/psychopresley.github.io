I. Top countries by active cases


The key indicator of the coronavirus outbreak is the number of daily active cases. This is the total number of confirmed cases, minus the reported number of deaths, minus the total recovered people in a given day. In other words it is the current number of people with the coronavirus disease. The chart below shows the top 10 countries by active cases through time.
